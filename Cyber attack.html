Types of Cyber Attacks

Phishing: Fake emails or websites that steal personal information.
Malware: Harmful software that damages or steals data.
Ransomware: Locks files until ransom is paid.
DDoS: Overloading servers to crash websites.

Prevention Tips

  • Use strong and unique passwords
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Keep software and systems updated
  • Avoid clicking suspicious links

Protection Tools

Use Firewalls, VPNs, Antivirus software, Encryption methods, and regular data backups to protect your systems.

Awareness

Organize training, campaigns, and awareness programs. Report cyber crimes at cybercrime.gov.in.
